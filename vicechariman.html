ABOUT VICE CHAIRMAN

Dr. K. Indira Veni

Dr. K. Indira Veni is an educationist, philanthropist and a business person As the Secretary of Jaya Educational Society, Vice-Chairperson of the Siddharth Group of Institutions and Chairman of Siddharth International Incubation Center, she has been spearheading innovative approaches in Engineering and Management education and is playing a pivotal role in the running of the institutions. She has also been the Proprietor of Indasri fuel and service center, Srikalahasti and a Partner in Elite Tours & Travels, Tirupati. An Electronics and Communication Engineer by education, and a doctorate from Sunrise University, Dr. Indira Veni believes in continuous education and is pursuing her LLB from Osmania University, Hyderabad. A successful mother of two successful children, she strongly believes in common good of the society and strives to help the needy.
